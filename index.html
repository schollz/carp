carp is a sequencer for chord arpeggios (carp). enter the chords you want with the arp you want inside parentheses, e.g. Bbmin(ud). hit "start" and it will play the corresponding chord and corresponding arp using sysex commands.


allowed chords:

maj (or nothing)
major
min (or m)
minor
sus
sustained
aug
augmented
dim
diminished

allowed arps:

u
up
d
down
ud
up-down
du
down-up
c
converge
v
diverge
cv
converge-diverge
vc
diverge-converge
r
random
s
stochastic

examples:

four chord song
C(u) Am(d) F(u) G(d)
